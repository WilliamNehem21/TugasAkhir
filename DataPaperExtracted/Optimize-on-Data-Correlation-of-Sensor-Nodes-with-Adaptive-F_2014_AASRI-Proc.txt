Conclusions Energy consumption and event monitoring has been one of the important indices for wireless sensor network performance .In this paper, AFTA is proposed. This approach  require data time correlation to fault tolerance through Threshold area. The proposed ap proach also determines the combination of algorithm and A - SMAC protocol. It is also shown that the AFTA improve the ruling data transmission efficiency effectively, makes the system has a higher real-time performance and reliability is better.
