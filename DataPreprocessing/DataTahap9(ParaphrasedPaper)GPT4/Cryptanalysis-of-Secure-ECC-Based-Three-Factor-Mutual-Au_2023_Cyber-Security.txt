C. Madan Kumar completed his B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering in 2004 at Dr. Paul Raj Engineering College affiliated to JNT University in Hyderabad, India. He then earned an M.Tech. in Software Engineering from Vaagdevi College of Engineering, also associated with JNT University Hyderabad, in 2012. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. at NIT Tiruchirappalli. Professionally, Kumar serves as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Kakatiya Institute of Technology and Science in Warangal, Telangana, India. His research interests span various aspects of security, including multimedia security, cryptography, network security, cryptanalysis, authentication protocols, as well as security in wireless sensor networks (WSN), the Internet of Things (IoT), and image security.
