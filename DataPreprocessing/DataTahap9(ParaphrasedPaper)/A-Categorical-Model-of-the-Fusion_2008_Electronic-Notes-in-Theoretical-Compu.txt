The description of bf-bisimulations outlined in proposition 4.6 enables us to juxtapose them with the original concepts of fusion bisimulation and hyperbisimulation as defined in section 2.2. Proposition 4.6 clauses (i) and (ii) encompass the communication action specifications in section 2.2. Additionally, the stipulation concerning bound actions in section 2.2 is reflected in clause (ii) through the requirement that, following a bound transition, the resultant processes are bisimilar under a distinct relation, utilizing a set of names where the newly allocated name has been utilized.
