The paper by J. Wang, K. Shang, X. Guo, X. Xia, and L. Da presents an innovative and efficient method for enhancing the production of short-chain fatty acids through anaerobic fermentation of waste activated sludge. This method involves the use of ferrous iron catalyzed peracetic acid as a pretreatment. The study was published in the Journal of Chemical Technology & Biotechnology in 2020, with the details available in volume 95, issue 3, pages 567-576.
