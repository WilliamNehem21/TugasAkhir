Madan Kumar obtained his B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from Dr. Paul Raj Engineering College (JNT University), Hyderabad, India in 2004. Following this, he completed his M.Tech. in Software Engineering at Vaagdevi College of Engineering (JNT University, Hyderabad) in 2012. Currently, he is pursuing his Ph.D. at NIT Tiruchirappalli. Madan Kumar is employed as an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Kakatiya Institute of Technology and Science in Warangal, Telangana, India. His research interests encompass multimedia security, cryptography, network security, cryptanalytic attacks, authentication protocols, WSN security, IoT security, and image security.
