The micro stereo vision system has become a prevalent tool in microscopic research areas, offering high-precision 3D measurement capabilities and visual feedback that are critical for detailed observation and analysis, applicable to both scientific and industrial purposes. This research successfully captured 3D information of objects under microscopic inspection by integrating phase shift technology with optical microscopy methods. The paper centers on the phase shift technology approach, detailing the development of a prototype system and its validation through extensive application testing in numerous fields, confirming the system's precision, practicality, and consistency.
